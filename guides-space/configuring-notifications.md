# Configuring Notifications

### Overview

Syncly's notification system keeps you informed without overwhelming your inbox. You can tailor alerts by channel, timing, and event type.

### Step 1: Open Notification Settings

* Click your avatar in the top-right corner and select **Settings**.
* Navigate to the **Notifications** tab.

### Step 2: Choose Your Channels

Select which channels you want to receive alerts on:

* **In-app** — a banner appears inside Syncly when an event is approaching.
* **Email** — a digest or per-event email is sent to your registered address.
* **SMS** — a text message is sent to your verified phone number (requires a verified number in Account Settings).

### Step 3: Set Default Reminder Timing

* Under **Event Reminders**, choose how far in advance you want to be notified: 5 minutes, 15 minutes, 30 minutes, 1 hour, or 1 day.
* You can override this default for any individual event when creating or editing it.

### Step 4: Manage Team Notification Preferences

* If you're a team admin, go to **Team Settings** > **Notifications** to set org-wide defaults.
* Individual members can override org defaults from their personal Settings page.

### Tips

* Use **Do Not Disturb** hours (Settings → Notifications → Quiet Hours) to pause alerts outside of working hours.
* For high-priority events, add multiple reminders (e.g. 1 day before and 30 minutes before) directly on the event.
