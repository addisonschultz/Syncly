# Setting Up Team Calendars

### Overview

Team calendars let your whole organization stay aligned on events, deadlines, and availability — all in one place.

### Step 1: Create a Team Calendar

* From the sidebar, click **Calendars** and select **New Calendar**.
* Give the calendar a name (e.g. "Engineering Team") and choose a color.
* Set the visibility to **Team** so all invited members can view it.

### Step 2: Invite Team Members

* Open the calendar settings by clicking the **···** menu next to the calendar name.
* Select **Share** and enter the email addresses of the people you want to invite.
* Assign each person a role: **Viewer** (read-only) or **Editor** (can create and edit events).

### Step 3: Add Events to the Shared Calendar

* Click **+ New Event** and select your team calendar from the **Calendar** dropdown.
* Add a title, time, location, and any relevant notes.
* Invited members will receive a notification and the event will appear on their Syncly dashboard.

### Tips

* Use the **Agenda** view to see a chronological list of upcoming team events.
* Color-code multiple team calendars to quickly distinguish between them.
* Enable **Conflicts Detection** in Settings to avoid double-booking team members.
